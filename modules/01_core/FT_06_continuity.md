## FT-6 — Champtique Continuity: Redistribution, Not Dissipation
**Status:** ACQUIRED
**Expression:** `∂tρ + ∇·J = S − R`

---

### Statement

Field density evolves through sources (local organization)
and redistribution outside the observation perimeter.
Total field Φ is conserved.

---

### Parameters

**S** — source: local organization, densification
**R** — redistribution outside the observation perimeter

---

### Critical Correction

**R ≠ dissipation.**

Dissipation does not exist in Pch.
What classical physics calls "loss" or "dissipation"
is redistribution of the field outside the observed system.
Total Φ is conserved. Return is always possible through field plasticity.

The growing entropy of classical thermodynamics is an observer artifact —
it reflects the limits of the measurement perimeter,
not a fundamental property of reality.

---

### Observable Grounding

- PH-046 (entropy): the Mpemba effect — documented since Aristotle —
  is structurally incompatible with entropy as a universal law.
  A hot system reaches lower energy faster than a cold one:
  redistribution, not irreversible dissipation.
- PH-016 (Oxford Electric Bell): oscillating since ~1840
  without measurable energy consumption — internal field redistribution
  in a closed system, no dissipation.
- EXP-005 to EXP-009: thermal signatures as redistribution fronts,
  not energy destruction.

---

### Falsification Criterion

FT-6 fails if:
irreversible total field destruction is demonstrated experimentally —
not redistribution outside the measurement perimeter,
but genuine annihilation of Φ.
