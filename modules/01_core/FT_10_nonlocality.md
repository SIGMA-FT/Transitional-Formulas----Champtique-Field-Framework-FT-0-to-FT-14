## FT-10 — Structural Non-Locality: Φd Precedes Φc
**Status:** ACQUIRED
**Expression:**
```
Matter     : local  (Φc — stabilized condensed field)
Information: non-local (Φd — extended diffuse field)
```

---

### Statement

Locality is a consequence of stabilization — not a fundamental property of reality.

The diffuse field Φd is structurally non-local:
it precedes Φc systematically and is accessible independently
of the geometric configuration of condensed matter.

---

### Discriminating Value

FT-10 has the highest discriminating value in the CMRPCh corpus.
It is the principal structural differential between Pch and the standard framework,
which treats locality as a default property of physical systems.

---

### Observable Grounding

- EXP-002 (Lewin capacitor): field maintained in relation between separated armatures
  — field is not stored in the matter of the armatures but maintained between them.
  Armatures separated: neutral to the touch.
  Reassembled: full charge present. The field was never in the matter.
- OBS-007 (zebras): geographic information accessible without social or genetic transmission
  — Φd inscription in territory, read non-locally by animals.
- EXP-013 (silver coin): peripheral Φd field detectable around coin
  without contact — non-local field structure around Φc substrate.

---

### Falsification Criterion

FT-10 fails if:
Φd is demonstrated to consistently follow Φc in all documented cases —
i.e., the diffuse field never precedes or exists independently
of a locally present condensed substrate.
