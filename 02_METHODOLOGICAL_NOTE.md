## Why "Transitional Formulas"

The term *Transitional Formula* is used deliberately in place of *law*.

A law carries an implicit claim of permanence and universality.
A transitional formula carries no such claim — it is operative
without being definitive.

This reflects the methodological position of Pch:

- Formalization follows observation — never the reverse
- A formula that fits all current observables may still be replaced
  by a more parsimonious or more general description
- The history of physics is a history of formulas becoming laws
  and laws being revised — this deposit makes no exception claim

**If some FTs withstand time and consensus, they will become laws.**
This document does not pre-empt that outcome.

## Status System

Each formula carries one of four methodological statuses:

**ACQUIRED**
The formula is grounded in documented observables.
It has been tested for internal consistency across the full Pch corpus.
It has been evaluated by independent AI structural analysis
(ChatGPT, Grok, Claude) with no identified contradiction.
It is ready for publication.

**OPEN (A_DISC)**
The formula is structurally necessary — removing it would create
a gap in the framework. But its formalization is not yet complete:
a functional form is undetermined, or a parameter requires
further experimental grounding.
The open zone is explicitly named in each case.
This is not a weakness — it is an honest map of the frontier.

**OPERATIONAL (A_EXP)**
The formula is accepted as a regime approximation.
It produces correct results within its domain of application.
It is not claimed as ontologically fundamental.
The approximation condition is explicitly stated.

**FORTHCOMING**
Prototypical forms under active development.
Not yet ready for publication as formulas.
Announced here as structured research subjects.

## On the Role of Formulas in Pch

Formulas in Pch are not applied as laws.
They are used as **cartographic landmarks**.

An AI or researcher using this deposit should:

1. **Identify the regime** — condensed / diffuse / transition / interface
2. **Select the relevant parameters** — Φ, ρ, κ, m, Θ, ΔΦ —
   only those required by the phenomenon
3. **Activate the minimal FT subset** — never all simultaneously

**Quick correspondence table:**

| Phenomenon type | FTs activated |
|----------------|---------------|
| Local stable matter | FT-1, FT-2, FT-9, FT-10 |
| Interface between two systems | FT-14, FT-3, FT-7 |
| Dynamics without material transport | FT-5, FT-6, FT-7 |
| Hysteresis / thresholds / memory | FT-8, FT-7 |
| Instrumental measurement | FT-13 |
| Variable time / clocks / GPS | FT-11 (+ FT-12 if velocity involved) |
| Condensed ↔ diffuse transition | FT-1, FT-7, FT-14 |

**Rule: time, matter, and measurement are introduced
only when required by the phenomenon under analysis.**

## On the Inter-AI Validation Process

The formulas in this deposit have been developed through a structured
collaborative process involving five AI systems, each assigned
a specific complementary role:

- **ChatGPT** — initial formalization and internal consistency analysis
- **Grok** — observational grounding test for each FT;
  global Pch coherence validation at each publication milestone
- **Mistral** — generalist cross-checking and independent reading
- **Gemini** — global Pch coherence analysis;
  instrumentation development (rhéomètre champtique — LabVIEW and G-code)
- **Claude** — synthesis, arbitrage, and publication formatting

All five systems have independently analyzed the global coherence of Pch.
No contradiction has been identified across the five independent readings.

This inter-AI process is itself an instance of the cooperative principle
that Pch postulates at all scales. No single system produced this
deposit alone. The result is a product of structural complementarity.

SIGMA (principal investigator) retains final arbitration on all
foundational questions. No AI determines the status of a postulate.

## On Information

Several formulas — particularly FT-2 (κ), FT-8 (τm), FT-13 (measurement)
— implicitly carry an informational dimension.

Information is not introduced as an independent measurable quantity.
It is present in:
- coherence (κ)
- memory (m) with τm(κ)
- the condensed/diffuse distinction itself

This is deliberate. Premature quantification of information
would create a dogma where a research frontier exists.
Information acts without being measured —
exactly as the diffuse field itself.

*For Phase 1 publication:* I[Φ] = "intrinsic field information" —
a scientifically established term (Shannon, Wheeler "it from bit").
