## Objective

Document the minimal formal structure of the single-field framework
developed across CMRPCh Deposits 1–4.

Fifteen transitional formulas (FT-0 to FT-14) are presented with:
- Expression and parameters
- Methodological status (ACQUIRED / OPEN / OPERATIONAL)
- Observable grounding
- Links to discriminating experiments and documented phenomena
- Falsification criterion

---

## Why "Transitional"

These formulas are intentionally named *transitional*, not *laws*.

This reflects a deliberate methodological position:

- Every formalization is historical and contextual
- A formula can be operative without being definitive
- Scientific consensus evolves
- Every FT remains open to correction, simplification, or replacement

If some FTs withstand time and consensus, they may eventually be
recognized as laws. This document makes no such assumption.
