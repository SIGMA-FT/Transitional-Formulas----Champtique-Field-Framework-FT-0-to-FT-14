**Description (for Zenodo abstract field):**

This deposit documents the minimal formal structure of the
single-field champtique framework (Pch) developed across
CMRPCh Deposits 1–4.

Fifteen transitional formulas (FT-0 to FT-14) are organized
into four methodological categories: ACQUIRED (11), OPEN (3),
OPERATIONAL (1), FORTHCOMING.

Each formula is presented with expression, parameters,
observational grounding, falsification criterion,
and links to previous deposits.

An inter-AI constants extraction program is announced.
